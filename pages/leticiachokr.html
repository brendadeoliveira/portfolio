Author Leticia Chokr

Author Leticia Chokr is a React Application for an author to present and sell her books. I am building this project from scratch to practice the language.

Project Purpose and Goal

The project will have 2 parts:

Part 1: June 2022 - The static website to present the author, her books and ways to buy the digital version and the option to buy the paperback, with a cart and automatic calculation for domestic shipping (in Brazil).

Part 2: July 2022 - Creating book worlds for each book: book one is a murder mystery so clients will be able to solve a murder in the website using clues from the book and the author's other social medias. Book two is about Ballet and Hockey so customers will be able to play around each of the main characters' schedules at the University.

Since this is the first real world website I am making, I am having trouble keeping my code organized in ways that months from now I will understand and be able to keep adding to her website.

Furthermore, in Brazil there is another way to make payments: printing out the bill and paying it at a store, which is very popular with most customers and since I do not live there I had a difficult time understanding how the payment options would work.
